About William Tame

I specialize in guiding students through the academic and social aspects of learning English as an additional language (EAL).

As a Global EAL Collaboration Leader, I'm experienced at coordinating language assessment, implementing effective teaching strategies, and facilitating professional learning opportunities.

My rich teaching experience spanning over 14 years in teaching EAL within international school settings has equipped me with a deep understanding of diverse learning needs.

"Once upon a time..."

I possess a remarkable skill for storytelling, especially when engaging with young students. Through the magic of words, I create vibrant and captivating narratives that not only entertain but also inspire young minds.

What sets me apart is my ability to enhance storytelling with quick, vivid illustrations that bring the narrative to life. I can seamlessly transition from words to visuals, providing a multi-sensory experience for my audience. Whether it's weaving tales of adventure, instilling valuable life lessons, or sparking the imagination with fantastical worlds, I draw quick illustrations to help visualize the story.

By incorporating interactive elements and encouraging creativity, I strive to make storytelling an enriching and memorable experience for young learners, fostering a love for literature and the joy of exploration within their hearts."
